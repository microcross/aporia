## Reading & Navigation UX

| Status | Idea | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 💡 | **Image hover previews in chat** | Hovering over an image reference surfaces a preview pop-over inline in the chat — like link previews on Wikipedia. Lets users glance at figures/diagrams without leaving the flow of conversation. Pairs naturally with the pop-over sub-question pattern already core to the UX. |
| 💡 | **Wikipedia-style link previews** | Hovering over a link shows a preview card (title, summary, thumbnail) before clicking. Reinforces the "rabbit hole" nature of exploratory learning — users can gauge where a link leads and decide how deep to go without losing their place. |
